# Market Analyzer - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Navigate to Market Analyzer
1. Open AgriTech AI application
2. Click on **Market** in the navigation menu
3. You'll see the Market Analyzer dashboard

### Step 2: Upload a Crop Image
1. Click the **"Choose Image"** button
2. Select a photo of your crop from your device
   - Supports: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF
   - Maximum size: 10MB
3. Enter your **Farm Area** in hectares (default: 1)
4. Click **Upload** - the system will analyze automatically

### Step 3: View Analysis Results
Once uploaded, you'll see:

**📸 Analysis Tab:**
- Crop identified with confidence score
- Plant health score (0-100%)
- Any issues detected (pests, diseases, deficiencies)
- Top crop recommendation with investment score

**💹 Profit Breakdown:**
- Expected Revenue (₹)
- Estimated Cost (₹)
- Net Profit (₹)
- ROI Percentage

**📅 Seasonal Information:**
- Best time to plant
- Optimal months for this crop
- Current season status

## 📈 Profit Calculation Explained

### How Profit is Calculated

**Formula:**
```
Profit = (Crop Price × Yield × Area) - (Cost × Area)
ROI = (Profit / Total Cost) × 100
```

**Example for Rice (1 hectare):**
- Average Price: ₹2,500 per quintal
- Yield: 50 quintals per hectare
- **Revenue:** ₹2,500 × 50 = ₹125,000
- **Cost:** ₹50,000
- **Profit:** ₹125,000 - ₹50,000 = **₹75,000**
- **ROI:** (₹75,000 / ₹50,000) × 100 = **150%**

**For 2 Hectares:**
- Revenue: ₹125,000 × 2 = ₹250,000
- Cost: ₹50,000 × 2 = ₹100,000
- Profit: ₹150,000
- ROI: 150% (same percentage!)

### Data Sources
- **Prices:** Real mandi rates from Government sites
- **Yields:** Average productivity data
- **Costs:** Realistic farming expenses
- **Updated:** Monthly with market changes

- Realistic farming costs
- Your specific farm area

### Q: How often is price data updated?
**A:**

### Q: Why are some crops not recommended?

- Better alternatives available

### Q: Can I compare crops from different seasons?

### Q: How do I improve my farm profit?
**A:**
1. Follow market analyzer recommendations
2. Plant during optimal seasons
3. Monitor profit scores
4. Diversify with 2-3 profitable crops
5. Use seasonal rotations
