## 🎓 Key Learning: Why Both Technologies?

### DSSAT Strengths
- Physics-based crop simulation
- Pre-season planning & optimization
- Predictable outputs
- Benchmarked accuracy (40+ years)

### DSSAT Limitations
- Doesn't see actual field conditions
- Can't detect anomalies
- No real-time monitoring
- Generic (not field-specific)

### FarmVibes.AI Strengths
- Real-time satellite monitoring
- Actual field conditions
- Anomaly detection
- Hyperlocal data

### FarmVibes.AI Limitations
- Doesn't predict future
- Weather-dependent (clouds)
- No crop simulation
- Needs historical context

### Combined Approach
- DSSAT: "Here's what should happen"
- FarmVibes: "Here's what's actually happening"
- Together: Precise, accurate, science-backed
